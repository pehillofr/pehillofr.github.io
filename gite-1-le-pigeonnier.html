Vacation Rental 1

The first bed and breakfast can accommodate up to 6 people (8 if using the pull out couch) and features:

  • One master bedroom with a private bathroom and shower
  • 2 additional bedrooms with a shared bathroom and shower
  • A fully equipped kitchen and living room with a pull out couch
  • A private outdoor terrace and garden to enjoy meals outdoors while overlooking the countryside
  • Swimming pool access from May to October
  • WiFi
  • Pets are not allowed


*Renting the full bed and breakfast during high and very high seasons can only be done for a minimum of 1 week from Saturday to Saturday.



Renting 1-3 rooms in the bed and breakfast from September to June is more flexible:

  • Medium season: book by night or by week
  • Low season: by night or for multiple months
