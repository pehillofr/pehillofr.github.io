Things To Do and See

When you arrive at Pehillo, our property managers, Sonia and Michel, will be there to not only welcome you but also to give you an overview of everything there is to do in the region. From visits to local markets and villages, hiking in the countryside and tasting the best local dishes - Sonia and Michel will help you curate the perfect stay.

Here's what you can expect from your time in the Lot-et-Garonne:

Visit Nerac & surrounding villages and cities

About a 5 minute drive from Pehillo, you'll find the charming historic city of Nerac where you can easily spend a few days visiting King Henry IV's castle and its museum, trying local cuisine, boating along the Baise river, exploring the traditional Saturday local market and simply strolling up and down the city's beautiful narrow streets.

Pehillo is also located close to many beautiful cities and villages ideal for day trips including Bordeaux, Toulouse, Condom, Fources, Larressingle, Saint-Emilion and many more. All of these cities will allow you to explore different parts of the culture and traditions of the Southwest of France.

Wine Country

About an hour and a half drive north of Pehillo, you'll find 5 famous wine trails called the Bordeaux Wine routes. This 55 mile route is filled with beautiful historic castles and vineyards giving you the opportunity to visit one of the world's best known territory for fine wine.

Sporting Activities

Want to stay active during your vacation? You can start by exploring the walking trail inside the property to see local wildlife and greenery.In Nerac and the surrounding area, you'll find opportunities to bike, zip line, hike, play tennis and horseback ride.

Water Parks

The region's largest water parks is about a 30 minute drive from Pehillo - from wave pools to water slides and everything in between, there's no doubt you'll find refreshing fun for the whole family.

Spas & Thermal Baths

The region is known for its thermal baths, a tradition in the Lot-et-Garonne since the Roman times. Many of them offer full spa services that makes for a great day trip to relax and unwind during your vacation.
